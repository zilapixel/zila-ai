AI Integration Plan

Our AI integration strategy involves three core components: market analytics engine, sentiment analysis system, and predictive modeling framework. These tools will provide unprecedented insights to ZILA holders.

The AI dashboard will feature real-time market analysis, social sentiment tracking, price prediction models, and automated trading signals. All AI features will be accessible exclusively to ZILA token holders.

Governance Structure

ZILA operates as a Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO), where token holders have direct voting rights on all major decisions. Proposals can be submitted by any holder meeting the minimum token threshold.

Voting power is proportional to token holdings, with quadratic voting mechanisms to prevent whale dominance. The treasury is managed collectively, with all expenditures requiring community approval.
